Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our career counselling services

Career counselling is a professional service that helps you understand your strengths, interests, and aptitudes to make informed career decisions. Our counsellors use scientific assessments, one-on-one discussions, and industry insights to guide you towards a fulfilling career path that aligns with your unique profile.

Our services are valuable for students choosing streams after 10th or 12th grade, college students selecting majors, fresh graduates entering the job market, and professionals considering career changes or advancement. Anyone seeking clarity about their career path can benefit from our guidance.

Our process typically includes: initial consultation to understand your background and goals, comprehensive aptitude and personality assessments, detailed analysis of results, one-on-one counselling sessions to discuss career options, and a personalized action plan with recommended steps and resources.

The duration varies based on individual needs. Typically, the assessment and initial counselling sessions take 2-3 hours, spread over a week. However, career planning is an ongoing journey, and we provide follow-up support as needed throughout your career development.
